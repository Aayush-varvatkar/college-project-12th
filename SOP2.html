STEPHEN HAWKING

  • Name: Stephen William Hawking
  • Date of birth: 8 January 1942
  • Birth place: Oxford, England
  • Date of death: 14 March 2018

INFORMATION ABOUT STEPHEN HAWKING

  • Hawking was born in Oxford, into a family of physicians.
  • A Brief History of Time appeared on the Sunday Times bestseller list for a record-breaking 237 weeks.
  • In 2002, Hawking was ranked number 25 in the BBC's poll of the 100 Greatest Britons.
  • He died on 14 March 2018 at the age of 76, after living with motor neurone disease for more than 50 years.
